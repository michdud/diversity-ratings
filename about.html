About Ratings of Inclusion

Why a database about diversity and positive representation?

People of color, women, people with disabilities, people in the LGBT+ community, and people at the intersections of these identities are underrepresented, or worse, completely misrepresented in media. Since we consume media almost constantly, it influences our views of others around us. While this is not necessarily a bad thing, when the media is controlled by a small subset of the population, we only see a small portion of perspectives about different groups. We need diverse media so that many, not few, stories are told.
